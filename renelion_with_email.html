Battery Energy Storage Systems

BESS ENGINEERING & CONSULTING

From a single-site industrial BESS to a multi-MW grid-scale project, we handle every technical and commercial layer — ensuring your system is correctly sized, financially optimised, and tender-ready.

Our BESS team brings experience across lithium-ion (LFP, NMC), flow batteries, and hybrid chemistries. We understand real-world constraints — thermal management, cycle degradation, grid codes — and we build those into every deliverable.
BESS Sizing & Design
MW/MWh sizing from load profiles, power flow analysis, duration optimisation, technology selection and configuration design.
BEDPA
Battery Energy Detailed Project Analysis — comprehensive technical studies covering degradation, round-trip efficiency, and lifetime performance.
Tender Documentation
RFP/RFQ preparation, technical specifications, scope of work, data sheets, vendor shortlisting, and bid evaluation.
Detailed Calculations
Electrical load flow, short circuit studies, protection coordination, harmonic analysis, and cable sizing for BESS integration.
Financial Modelling
CAPEX/OPEX modelling, revenue stacking (arbitrage, ancillary, capacity), IRR/NPV/payback calculations, and bankable reports.
Performance Optimisation
Dispatch strategy development, SoC management, cycling optimisation, and real-time performance benchmarking for operating assets.
Regulatory & Grid Compliance
Grid code compliance review, interconnection studies, CEIG/CEA regulation guidance, and utility coordination support.
Project Planning & Feasibility
Site assessment, technology comparison, risk analysis, implementation roadmap, and go/no-go feasibility studies.
Solar PV Systems

SOLAR PV ENGINEERING & CONSULTING

Comprehensive solar PV consulting for utility-scale, C&I, and rooftop projects — combining PVsyst modelling, grid engineering, and rigorous financial analysis.

Our solar engineers have delivered projects across multiple states in India and are experienced in DISCOM interconnection, CEIG/CEA approvals, and RPO compliance. We go beyond reports — we deliver documents you can submit.
Energy Yield Analysis
PVsyst-based P50/P90 generation modelling, shadow analysis, soiling loss estimation, and long-term performance forecasting.
System Sizing & Design
DC/AC ratio optimisation, inverter and module selection, string configuration, layout design, and irradiation studies.
Tender Documentation
Technical specifications, BoQ preparation, bid documents, vendor evaluation matrices, and contract technical annexures.
Detailed Calculations
Cable sizing, earthing design, protection relay coordination, transformer sizing, and grid integration studies (load flow, fault level).
Financial Modelling
LCOE calculation, tariff benchmarking, PPA structuring support, debt/equity modelling, and IRR sensitivity analysis.
Grid Integration
SLDC/RLDC interface, scheduling & forecasting obligations, reactive power management, and inter-state transmission planning.
Regulatory & Approvals
CEIG/CEA inspection readiness, DISCOM net-metering documentation, RPO compliance reports, and statutory submissions.
O&M Advisory
Performance ratio benchmarking, O&M contract review, IV curve analysis, thermography interpretation, and degradation tracking.
Integrated BESS + Solar PV

HYBRID SOLAR + STORAGE SYSTEMS

The most complex — and most valuable — energy systems require both BESS and solar expertise working in tandem. Renelion delivers fully integrated hybrid project consulting.

Hybrid projects demand a rare combination of storage chemistry knowledge, solar yield modelling, and grid dispatch expertise. Our cross-domain team handles it as a single, coherent engagement.
Co-Location Design
Shared infrastructure planning — transformers, switchgear, interconnection — for solar and BESS on the same site, minimising CAPEX.
Dispatch Strategy
Revenue-maximising dispatch algorithms — time-of-use arbitrage, peak shaving, ancillary services, and solar firming strategies.
Integrated Sizing
Optimisation of PV capacity and BESS duration together — iterative modelling to find the commercial sweet spot for your use case.
Bankable Feasibility Reports
Lender-grade technical and financial reports including independent engineer (IE) quality assumptions for project finance.
Tender & Procurement
Fully integrated tender packages covering both PV and BESS scopes — single-point vendor or split-contract approaches.
Financial Modelling
Hybrid revenue stack modelling — combining solar generation revenue, BESS ancillary income, capacity payments, and green certificates.
Grid Code Compliance
Ensuring hybrid systems meet IEGC, SERC, and CEA requirements — forecasting obligations, reactive capability, and protection settings.
Performance Monitoring
Integrated SCADA/EMS advisory, KPI dashboards, actual-vs-model benchmarking, and intervention recommendations for live projects.
